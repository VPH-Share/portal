Help



The VPH-Share team is fully committed to provide the most efficient and effective support to our users. We have prepared for this a highly detailed User guide with a description on how to use all VPH-Share portal functionalities. To visit the User's guide please click here.

How to send a feedback
We are looking for bugs reporting, and suggestions for improvements from all our users. These feedbacks will be used to keep updated a page with the issues already reported and fixed publicly available.

If you would like to report any issue just drop an email to support@vph-share.eu and the technical team will be back to you as soon as possible!
