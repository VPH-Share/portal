About Us

VPH-Share is an online environment for the construction of biomedical workflows. It is designed to help researchers, developers and clinicians quickly and easily share biomedical tools and data, to simplify and speed up the process. VPH-Share was created through a four-year, European FP7 funded project, which brought together 21 international partners from industry, academia and healthcare.
